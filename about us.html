About Our Studio

Welcome to our nail studio! We are a team of professional nail technicians dedicated to providing the best nail care services in town. Our goal is to make you feel confident and beautiful with every visit.

Our Story

Tebo's nail heaven was created in early 2025 by Tebo Dimakatso. Tebo's vision was to create a welcoming and inclusive space where everyone could enjoy high-quality nail services. With a commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction, Tebo's nail heaven has quickly become a favorite destination for nail care enthusiasts. We specialize in elegant nail designs, acrylics,gel nails, and pedicures. Our team is dedicated to staying up-to-date with the latest trends and techniques in nail art to ensure that our clients receive the best possible service. We believe that beautiful nails can boost confidence and enhance your overall style, and we are here to help you achieve that. Thank you for choosing Tebo's nail heaven, where your nails are our passion!
