Nailcare Tips

Learn how to keep your nails healthy, strong and beautiful.

Moisturize Your Cuticles

Apply cuticle oil daily to keep your nails hydrated and healthy.

Avoid Using Nails As Tools

Avoid opening cans or scratching hard surfaces with your nails to prevent breakage.

Wear Gloves When Cleaning

Cleaning chemicals can damage your nails and weaken nail polish.

More Nailcare Advice

Stay Hydrated

Drinking water helps maintain healthy nails.

Book Refills Regularly

Regular maintenance keeps acrylics fresh.

Do Not Peel Off Nails

Peeling acrylics damages natural nails.
