General Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of nail services including manicures, pedicures, gel nails, acrylic nails, and more. Please visit our Services page for a full list of offerings.

How can I book an appointment?

You can book an appointment through our Book Now page or by contacting us directly via email or phone.

What are your operating hours?

We are open Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 7:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays.

Do you offer walk-in services?

Yes, we do accept walk-ins based on availability. However, we recommend booking an appointment to ensure you get the time slot you prefer.

How much do you charge for your services?

Our prices vary depending on the service. Please visit our Services page for detailed pricing information.

What is your cancellation policy?

We require at least 24 hours' notice for cancellations. Cancellations made within 24 hours of the appointment may be subject to a fee.

Do you offer gift cards?

Yes, we offer gift cards that can be purchased in-store or online. They make a perfect gift for any occasion!

How long do your nail sets last?

Our nail sets typically last between 1-2 weeks, depending on your natural nail growth and daily activities. Regular maintenance is recommended to keep them looking their best. Please check our nail care tips page for long lasting tips and schedule a refill appointment to maintain your look!

What is your refund policy?

we strive to ensure our clients are satisfied with our services. If you are not happy with your nails, please contact us within 48 hours of your appointment, and we will do our best to address your concerns. Refunds may be issued at our discretion based on the situation.

Do you offer any discounts or promotions?

Yes, we frequently offer special promotions and discounts. Please check our Special Offers page for the latest deals and follow us on social media for updates.
