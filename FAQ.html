Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about our services.

How long do acrylic nails last?

Acrylic nails usually last 2 to 3 weeks before a refill is needed.

Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes, but appointments are recommended to avoid waiting.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book through our booking page or contact us directly.

Do you remove old acrylics?

Yes, we safely remove acrylics without damaging natural nails.

How should I care for my nails?

Moisturize your cuticles regularly and avoid using nails as tools.
